# Employee Benefits Guide 2026

## Health Insurance

### Medical Coverage
- Annual limit: $1,000,000 per person
- Deductible: $500 individual / $1,000 family
- Co-pay: $25 office visit / $50 specialist
- Prescription drugs: $10 generic / $30 brand-name / $50 specialty

### Dental Coverage
- Annual limit: $5,000 per person
- Preventive care: 100% covered (cleanings, exams, x-rays)
- Basic procedures: 80% covered (fillings, extractions)
- Major procedures: 50% covered (crowns, bridges, root canals)

### Vision Coverage
- Eye exam: $20 co-pay, once per year
- Glasses: $150 allowance every 2 years
- Contact lenses: $200 allowance every year

## Eligibility

| Employment Type | Waiting Period | Coverage Start |
|----------------|---------------|----------------|
| Full-time (30+ hrs/week) | 90 days | 1st of month after 90 days |
| Part-time (20-29 hrs/week) | 6 months | Limited medical only |
| Temporary/Contract | Not eligible | N/A |

## Enrollment Periods

- **New hire enrollment**: Within 30 days of start date
- **Open enrollment**: November 1-30 each year
- **Special enrollment**: 60 days after qualifying life event (marriage, birth, adoption, loss of other coverage)

## Dependents

Eligible dependents include:
- Spouse or domestic partner
- Children under age 26 (biological, adopted, stepchildren)
- Disabled children of any age if disability began before age 26

## How to Enroll

1. Log into the benefits portal at https://benefits.company.com
2. Select your coverage level (Individual / Employee + Spouse / Employee + Children / Family)
3. Choose your plan options
4. Submit before the deadline

Contact HR at benefits@company.com for questions.
